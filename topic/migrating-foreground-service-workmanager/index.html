Migrating from Foreground Service to WorkManager

Time:  Day 2 (2024-07-06) 12:30 - 12:45

Venue: MWT2

Language: English

Level: Beginner

Since Android 14, Google has officially recommended migrating from Foreground Services to WorkManager for dataSync-related jobs. This talk aims to share how this can be done for different use cases, issues that may arise, and workaround for them.

Aayush Gupta / India

Independent Contractor

Aayush Gupta (he/him), Android Developer and Developer Relations, is a self-taught developer, working on FOSS and various communities, prominently XDA Developers, for the past few years. He holds multiple titles in numerous communities, both personal and professional. Working remotely from Bhilai, India, he actively contributes to developing Android-related projects.
